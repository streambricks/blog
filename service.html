What services do we provide?

Expert-Led Apache Pulsar Deployment & Management
Our team, consisting of Apache Pulsar creators and experts with years of experience, helps companies deploy large-scale pub-sub messaging platforms. We provide a fully managed service capable of supporting millions of topics with low-latency SLAs, guaranteed durability, and high availability. Alongside deployment, we offer essential tools, high-quality operational management training, and expertise to ensure maximum system availability, smooth upgrades, and incident handling, minimizing disruptions in your production environment.

Flexible Deployment: On-Prem & Cloud
Whether on-premises or in the cloud, on VMs, bare-metal, or containers, we specialize in deploying and managing high-performance, durable, and highly available Pulsar clusters. Our cost-optimized deployment model can reduce costs by up to 5x compared to your existing messaging system, ensuring financial efficiency without compromising performance or reliability.

Cloud Deployment on AWS, GCP & Azure
Our cloud deployment solutions on AWS, GCP, and Azure can cut costs by up to 10x compared to native cloud messaging services. If you're new to cloud infrastructure, containers, or cloud-native ecosystems, our team provides hands-on guidance and training, enabling you to set up and manage a stable, production-ready Pulsar cluster on any environment with ease.

Custom Feature Development & On-Demand Releases
Have a specific messaging system requirement to support your unique use case? We offer on-demand feature development and stable version releases, ensuring that your system evolves with your needs and delivers the best experience to your users with the highest priority.
